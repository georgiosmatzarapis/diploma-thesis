  About Allergy Map - U.S.


Allergymapus.com is a web platform in which you are able to track your allergies by submitting your daily symptoms and also stay informed about past, current and future data which are related with allergies, pollen and humidity levels. In addition, you are given the ability to keep and update a personal profile with various allergy data and also your received medication.

Considering the part of allergy tracking, you can visit 'myHealth' from the navigation menu, where you can find the line graph of your health status and also a heatmap which contains the locations you have visited. Both features are depended on the submission of daily symptoms form, which is located at the home page and we highly recommend filling out.

At the home page, you can check allergy, pollen and humidity levels in the U.S.A. region. Starting with pollen, two color maps have been created by accessing pollen sensor values ​​located in the US geographical area. The first map gives the color of the areas based on the average of the pollen pollen levels, while the second one displays these values ​​based on the location of each sensor. The last map helps the user in order to know the distribution of the sensors and secondly to be able to better understand the intensity and density of the pollen levels. Then for the performance of the allergy levels, there are also two maps of the same format as the above, with the difference that they depict the symptom levels of the users of the platform. Furthermore, an additional map is provided in order to depict the number of tweets related to allergies and more focused on pollen allergies. Finally, two more hybrid maps are offered. The first depicts in combination the two subjective inputs of the system, users's symptoms of the platform and tweets, and the second depicts with appropriate weights all the above information.

The information provided by Allergymapus.com has a complementary role. This means that we do not intend to replace any advice given by a health professional. Furthermore, we respect the legal requirements, including those concerning medical and personal information privacy applying in the United States of America.
